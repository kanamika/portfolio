Skills

PCR

PCR means polymerase chain reaction. It’s a test to detect genetic material from a specific organism, such as a virus. The test detects the presence of a virus if you have the virus at the time of the test. The test could also detect fragments of the virus even after you are no longer infected.

Cromatography

Chromatography is a process for separating components of a mixture. The different components of the mixture travel through the stationary phase at different speeds, causing them to separate from one another.

Enzyme Purification

Enzyme purification is often a complex process, and a number of methods are usually applied in sequence to attain sufficiently high purity levels. Ultrafiltration is another technique used to separate enzymes, provided the molecular weight cutoff of the membrane is compatible with that of the target enzyme.
